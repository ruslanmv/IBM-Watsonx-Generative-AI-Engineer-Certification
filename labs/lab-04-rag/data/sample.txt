IBM watsonx Discovery is the managed hybrid retrieval service in the
watsonx family. Under the hood it runs BM25 keyword search and dense-vector
search over the same Elasticsearch index, then optionally re-ranks the
top results with a cross-encoder model. It integrates with watsonx.governance
for end-to-end lineage and drift monitoring.

Granite Guardian is the IBM family of small classifier models that detect
unsafe content in both user input and model output. Categories include
harm, social bias, profanity, sexual content, unethical behaviour, violence,
jailbreaks, and (for RAG) groundedness — i.e. is the answer supported by the
retrieved context?

For on-prem and air-gapped deployments, watsonx.ai is also available as part
of IBM Cloud Pak for Data (the Software offering), which can run in your own
VPC or data centre with the same APIs as the SaaS edition.
