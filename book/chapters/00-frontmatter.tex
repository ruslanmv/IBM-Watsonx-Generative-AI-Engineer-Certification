\chapter*{Preface}
\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Preface}

The field of Generative AI is rapidly transforming the technological landscape, and IBM's watsonx platform is at the forefront of this revolution. This comprehensive study guide and exam workbook is meticulously crafted to prepare you for the \textbf{IBM Watsonx Generative AI Engineer Associate (C1000-185)} certification exam.

\section*{Purpose of This Book}

This book is more than just a collection of facts; it is a structured learning path designed to build a solid foundation in Generative AI concepts, prompt engineering, fine-tuning, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), deployment, and orchestration, all within the context of the IBM watsonx ecosystem. We focus on practical, exam-oriented knowledge, ensuring that you not only understand the theory but also know how to apply it in real-world scenarios using IBM's tools like watsonx.ai and InstructLab.

\section*{Who This Book Is For}

This study guide is designed for:

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]

    \item \textbf{Data Scientists} expanding into generative AI implementation
    \item \textbf{Solution Architects} designing AI-powered enterprise solutions
    \item \textbf{Technical Professionals} seeking IBM certification credentials
    \item \textbf{Students and Self-Learners} building expertise in enterprise AI platforms
\end{itemize}
